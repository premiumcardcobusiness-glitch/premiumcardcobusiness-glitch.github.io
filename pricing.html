Plan details — please read before you buy

All plans are auto-renewing subscriptions: monthly plans renew every month, annual plans renew every 12 months, at the prices shown above, until you cancel. You can cancel anytime from your account or by emailing us — you keep access for the period you already paid for. Annual billing equals ten months' price for twelve months of access (two months free).

The free trial is 25 card scans inside the app — it requires no payment details, never converts into a paid plan automatically, and simply stops at 25 scans until you choose a plan. There is no other introductory pricing.

Taxes may apply and will be calculated at checkout. All prices shown in USD. Checkout is securely handled by Paddle, our authorized reseller and Merchant of Record.

What you need to run it

Beltstra Co is software. To use it you run our desktop application on your own computer with a camera. A companion physical scanning rig (3D-printed parts + a Raspberry Pi) is optional and built by you separately — it is not sold here.
