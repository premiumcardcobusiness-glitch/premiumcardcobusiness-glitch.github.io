Plan details — please read before you buy

All paid plans are auto-renewing subscriptions: monthly plans renew every month, annual plans renew every 12 months, at the prices shown above, until you cancel. You can cancel anytime from your account or by emailing us — you keep access for the period you already paid for. Annual billing equals ten months' price for twelve months of access (two months free).

Scan allowances are rolling windows, not calendar months: Pro includes 800 scans per rolling 30 days with a burst limit of 100 per rolling 24 hours; Studio includes 3,000 per 30 days and 400 per 24 hours. One scan = one card identified and priced. Your live usage is always visible inside the app. Unused scans don't roll over.

The free trial is 50 card scans over 7 days — it requires no payment details and does not convert into a paid plan automatically. When it ends, you simply choose a plan if you'd like to continue.

Taxes may apply and will be calculated at checkout. All prices in USD. Checkout is securely handled by Paddle, our authorized reseller and Merchant of Record.

What you need to run it

A phone or computer with a camera and a web browser. That's it — there is no app to download and no software to install. Card recognition runs on our servers; you sign in with your license token and scan.
