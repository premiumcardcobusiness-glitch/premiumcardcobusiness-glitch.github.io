Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Beltstra Co ("we", "us"), a sole proprietorship based in Aubrey, Texas, USA, collects and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

Payment information

We do not collect or store your card or bank details. All payments are processed by our Merchant of Record, Paddle.com, who collects the information needed to complete your purchase (such as name, email, billing country, and payment details) under their own privacy policy.

License information

To operate your membership we store a license identifier associated with your purchase and the email address you used, so we can issue and verify your license. When you sign in or scan, we receive your license token to confirm it is active.

Card images and usage

When you scan a card, the image is sent to our servers to identify the card and is deleted after processing — we do not keep your card photos. We record per-scan usage (a count, timestamp, and basic device/connection information) to enforce your plan's scan allowance and to detect account sharing or automated abuse.

2. How we use information

  • To issue, verify, and support your membership license
  • To respond to support requests you send us
  • To comply with legal and tax obligations (largely handled by Paddle)

3. Sharing

We share information only with service providers necessary to run the Service — primarily Paddle.com for payment processing. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data retention

We keep license and contact information for as long as your account is active and as needed to meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by emailing us. For payment data held by Paddle, contact Paddle directly.
